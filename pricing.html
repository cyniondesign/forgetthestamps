Pricing

 Only pay when you send emails

Our pricing structure couldn't be more simple. There are no monthly fees and no hidden fees - just the initial setup cost and then a low campaign delivery fee. For each campaign you send with more than 5 recipients, you pay a flat fee of GBP 6.00 plus 3 pence per message. Any campaigns you send to 5 or less people are free of charge.

Let's say you're sending an email newsletter to a database of 1,000 subscribers. To send this newsletter, you will be charged GBP 6.00 plus 3 pence for each recipient, GBP 30.00 in this case, making a total of GBP 36.00.

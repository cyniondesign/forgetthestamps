Features

With easy to use tools you can quickly add your own content, select the campaign recipients, and schedule the delivery.

Preview the email as you build it

Our live editor allows you to see your email take shape as you build it. You can add your own text and images to your template and even change the order any items might appear. The moment you add your own content we'll automatically save a draft so you can come back and finish it later if you run out of time.

Pick a template, any template

We can supply a selection of email templates for you. You might require a template for press or media releases and another for a monthly newsletter - it's completely up to you. Once setup you simply select which template you'd like to use and add your content accordingly.

Complete sender control

Your can use your own 'from name' and email address, so the email received always looks like it comes from you. In addition you can also select your own subject heading and which email address any replies should be forwarded to. We take all the messy work out of the equation by processing any bounces and unsubscribes automatically.

Send now or schedule for later

Once you're happy with the email, you can send yourself a test version to review your handiwork, then send it out to your recipients immediately or schedule it for delivery at another time. This way you can build your campaigns in advance and schedule them for delivery tomorrow, next month or any time in between.

Only pay when you send a campaign

Our unique pricing model means that you never pay any monthly or hidden fees and you only pay when you're actually sending a campaign. Testing a campaign is of course free!

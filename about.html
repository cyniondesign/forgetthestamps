About

The company behind Forget The Stamps

Forget The Stamps is a sophisticated yet simple to use web product brought to you by Cynion Design. We've been a small independent design company based in the UK since 1996. Forget The Stamps is just one of many products and services Cynion Design offer, enabling our customers to reach new markets and empower communications.

When you request an account on Forget The Stamps, an account manager will contact you to complete the process. When you need a design created, we'll work with you every step of the way. And when you email support@forgetthestamps.com, we'll be there to answer your questions and help you resolve any issues.
